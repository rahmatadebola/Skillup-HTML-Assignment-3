RECIPE FOR FRIED RICE

For two people

Ingredients

  • 3 cups of rice
  • 6 medium carrots
  • 1 tin green peas
  • 100g spring onions
  • Half kg of beef liver
  • 3 green peppers
  • 2 medium chicken laps
  • Curry, thyme, ginger and garlic taste
  • Seasoning cubes ,vegetable oil, 150g of butter
  • Salt to taste.
  • chicken stock

METHOD :

  1. Parboil the rice
  2. Preheat the oil in a pan on medium to high heat, throw in the sliced onions and fry for a minute or two
  3. Wash, scrape and dice the vegetables(carrots, green pepper etc).
  4. Fry the vegetables and keep aside
  5. Pour the chicken stock into a sieve then into a pot and set to boil.
  6. Once it boils, add the parboiled rice. Also add one tablespoon of plain yellow curry powder to colour the rice.
  7. Add salt to taste, thyme, salt, and seasoning cubes
  8. Cover and leave to cook . Monitor it till rice is soft and water is almost dry
  9. Open the tin of peas and pour into the pot of rice
  10. Pour in fried liver and all the vegetables.
  11. Stir carefully and properly
  12. Serve hot with fried chicken/turkey and chilled fruit juice.
